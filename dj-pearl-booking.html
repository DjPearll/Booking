About

Sound, curated with intention.

I've always believed the right song at the right moment can turn a room into a memory. That's the standard I hold every set to — reading the crowd, adjusting on the fly, and never coasting on autopilot. Whether it's a first dance or a dance floor that won't quit at 1am, I'm there to make sure the music does its job. My goal by the end of the night isn't for people to talk about the DJ — it's for them to remember how good it felt to be there.
